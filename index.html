About us

Muthu Finance, established in 1975 aims at meeting the financial needs of micro, small and medium enterprises which remained largely underserved despite several initiatives. We intend to become the leading financial services company that focuses on entrepreneurship.

With over 45 years of expertise, our committed specialists provide businesses and customers with one of the most convenient and distinctive financial services. Today, we are privileged to connect with thousands of customers in serving their needs.

Vision

To be a trustworthy financial organisation that appreciates client happiness and continually provides our consumers with quality services.

Mission

Confidence and integrity are our basic values. We look forward to our customers' long-term partnerships, which must be based on confidence and integrity. We aim at offering people and enterprises quality and inexpensive financial services. All our company factors are driven by these beliefs.
